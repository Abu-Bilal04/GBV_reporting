About Us

Your healing starts with being heard

We are a team committed to providing a safe, secure, and anonymous space for survivors of gender-based violence (GBV) to speak out and seek help. Our platform leverages decentralized technologies like the Cardano blockchain and IPFS to ensure that every report is permanently recorded without relying on a central authority. This protects your voice from being silenced, altered, or deleted — now or in the future. Beyond reporting, we also enable direct communication between survivors and verified therapists in a way that maintains privacy, respect, and confidentiality. By removing the need for personal accounts or revealing identities, we aim to restore power to survivors, giving them the choice to share their story, receive support, and begin their path to healing — all on their own terms.
