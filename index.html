About Us

Your healing starts with being heard

We are a team committed to providing a safe, secure, and anonymous space for survivors of gender-based violence (GBV) to speak out and seek help. Our platform leverages decentralized technologies like the Cardano blockchain and IPFS to ensure that every report is permanently recorded without relying on a central authority.

Beyond reporting, we enable direct communication between survivors and verified therapists, maintaining privacy, respect, and confidentiality. You are empowered to share your story and seek help — on your terms.
